Our Story

At You Not Cooking, we understand the rhythm of life in Pretoria Montana—the hard work, the community spirit, and the need for a truly satisfying meal at the end of a long day. Nestled right here in our vibrant neighborhood, we believe that every bite should be an experience, a bold and hearty journey crafted especially for our local workers and residents. We're not just serving food; we're serving up comfort, convenience, and culinary adventures designed to make you say, "Why cook when you can eat your heart out with us?"

Our menu is built for the hungry, featuring signature dishes like "The Rolls Royce (Luxury)" and "The G-Wagon (Heavy Duty)"—more than just sandwiches, they are generous, flavor-packed meals made with premium ingredients. From succulent beef patties and crispy bacon to double cheese and savory Russian sausages, each item is a testament to our philosophy: go big, go bold, and never compromise on taste or quality, right here in Pretoria Montana.

We know you work hard, and you deserve a meal that works just as hard for you. Whether you're grabbing a quick, delicious bite during your workday or bringing home a feast for the family after a busy day, You Not Cooking is your trusted local spot. Let us handle the cooking, so you can focus on what matters most. Your only job is to Eat Your Heart Out.

FAQ

What are your hours?

Tue-Thu 11AM-10PM, Fri-Sat 11AM-1AM, Sun 12PM-8PM. Monday we rest.

Do you deliver?

Yes. UberEats, Mr D, and our own riders within a 5km radius. R25 flat delivery fee for direct orders.

Can I book for a private event?

Absolutely. Check our Catering page for packages, or contact us for a custom quote.

How spicy is "The Regret" sauce?

Very. It's Carolina Reaper-based. We're not responsible for tears, existential crises, or aggressive WhatsApp messages sent afterwards.

Do you do merch returns?

Returns accepted within 14 days, unworn, with tags. No returns on food (obviously).

Do you have vegan options?

We have a plant-based smash patty and vegan-friendly loaded fries. Ask your server for the full list.
